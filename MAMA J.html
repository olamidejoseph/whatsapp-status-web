About Our Business

Welcome to MAMA J Frozen Foods

Located at Obor road behind court at amadike street Street, opposite Central Bank in the heart of the city, MAMA J has been providing quality frozen foods for over 10 years. We specialize in a wide variety of fresh fish, chicken, meat and eggs at competitive prices.

Our products range from ₦300 upwards, with prices varying according to size and quality. We offer both retail and wholesale options to meet all your needs.

At MAMA J, customer satisfaction is our priority. We ensure all our products are fresh, properly stored and delivered with care.
